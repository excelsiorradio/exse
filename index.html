About

Excelsior Southeast Communications was formed in 2019 to serve a regional need for quality radiosystems integrations. Even something as seemingly simple as radios can be so overwhelming in the “technical age.” That’s why we set out to make radio communications solutions more understandable, by helping businesses in and around our community create comprehensive communications plans that’ll perfectly fit their needs, without emptying their wallets. We also provide many programming and licensing solutions. Give us a call today!

FCC Licensing

Many businesses require some efficient means of communication across large areas. There are many factors involved in the paperwork for federal licensing. This is where Excelsior Southeast can help. We can consult for your agency/company, to simply assist in your independent completion of an application, or we can take the entire application process out of your hands and notify you upon successful processing. Contact us today for more information!

Radio Programming

Excelsior can program radios from countless major vendors, for everybody from amateur operators to government agencies. We can also work with your trunked and APCO-25 compliant systems!
